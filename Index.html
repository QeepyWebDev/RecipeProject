Sweet Rolls

For the Rolls:

  • ½tsp from Salt Pile
  • 1/4 cup from Jug of Milk
  • 2 cups from Sack of Flour
  • 2 sticks ( 1cup) of Butter
  • 5 Chicken's Eggs
  • 1½ tsp Extract from Vanilla Plant
  • 1½ cups of Moon Sugar (aka granulated sugar)
    • (You can add in 1 tsp of baking powder to assist in rising, but if you whip the butter and eggs enough, you won't need it.)
    Sweet Roll pan (mini bundt pan)

For the glaze:

  • 4 TB butter
  • 2 TB from Jug of Milk
  • 2 cups Powdered Moon Sugar (aka confectioner's sugar)
  • 1 tsp Extract from Vanilla Plant

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

  1. Gather your ingredients and allow to reach room temperature. Preheat your hearth to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together and beat until fluffy (at least five minutes).
  3. Slowly add in each chicken's egg and beat well. Add the milk or cream.
  4. Add the flour, salt, and vanilla extract and stir gently until mixed.
  5. Pour into greased Sweet Roll pans and bake until golden, about 20 minutes. Allow to cool.

Step 2: Glaze and Consume

  1. For the glaze, melt the butter and add in the milk.
  2. Slowly mix in the powdered sugar and vanilla. Drizzle over cooled Sweet Rolls.
  3. Do not fight any dragons for 30 minutes after consuming more than five Sweet Rolls.
